Mai Sakurajima is a character from the anime and light novel series "Seishun Buta Yarou wa Bunny Girl Senpai no Yume wo Minai" (commonly known as "Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai")

Age: High School student

Occupation: Former child actress and current high school student

Mai is known for wearing a bunny girl costume, which becomes a significant part of the series' plot. The costume symbolizes her struggle with being seen and recognized, and it becomes a visual representation of the series' exploration of identity and perception.
